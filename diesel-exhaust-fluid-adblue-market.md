<p><p>Diesel Exhaust Fluid (Adblue) is a non-hazardous solution made from a mixture of urea and deionized water. It is used in Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems in diesel vehicles to reduce nitrogen oxide (NOx) emissions and comply with environmental regulations. Adblue is stored in a separate tank in diesel vehicles and injected into the exhaust stream, converting harmful nitrogen oxide emissions into harmless nitrogen and water vapor.</p><p>The Diesel Exhaust Fluid (Adblue) Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Stringent emission regulations imposed by various governments around the world to reduce pollution levels are driving the demand for Adblue. The increasing adoption of SCR systems in diesel vehicles, ranging from passenger cars to heavy-duty trucks, is further boosting the market growth.</p><p>The market analysis reveals that the growing automotive industry, coupled with the rising demand for fuel-efficient vehicles, is also contributing to the expansion of the Adblue market.

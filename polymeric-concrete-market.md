<p><p>The polymeric concrete market encompasses three main types: aggregate, synthetic resin, and other additives. Aggregate refers to the solid materials like sand, gravel, or crushed stone used as fillers in the concrete mixture. Synthetic resin, on the other hand, serves as the bonding agent or adhesive that holds the aggregate together. It provides strength and durability to the concrete. Lastly, other additives are substances added to the concrete mixture to enhance specific properties such as flexibility, water resistance, or chemical resistance. These three components work together to create a high-performance polymeric concrete material.</p></p>

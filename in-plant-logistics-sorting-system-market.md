<p><p>In-plant logistics sorting systems refer to the equipment and processes used within a facility to organize and separate goods. There are two main types: horizontal cross band sorting system and vertical cross band sorting system. The horizontal system uses conveyors running perpendicular to the direction of goods flow, while the vertical system utilizes conveyors running along the vertical plane. Both types aim to efficiently sort and route items within a facility, streamlining operations and optimizing productivity.</p></p>

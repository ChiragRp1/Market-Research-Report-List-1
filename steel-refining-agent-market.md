<p><p>The steel refining agent market consists of three main types: hybrid, premelted, and sintered agents. Hybrid agents are a combination of different elements, such as aluminum and calcium, designed to refine and purify steel. Premelted agents are pre-made alloys that are added to the steel during the melting process to improve its properties. Sintered agents are made by sintering a mixture of ingredients and are commonly used in secondary refining processes. These agents play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and performance of steel.</p></p>

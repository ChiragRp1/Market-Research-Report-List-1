<p><p>Electric vehicle polyurethane adhesives are specialized adhesives used in the manufacturing and assembly of electric vehicles (EVs). The increasing demand for electric vehicles, driven by the growing concern for environmental sustainability and government regulations promoting EV adoption, is a key factor contributing to market growth. Additionally, the trend of lightweighting vehicles to improve fuel efficiency and range is also driving the demand for polyurethane adhesives as they offer superior bonding strength while reducing weight compared to traditional joining methods like welding or riveting.</p><p>Moreover, the shift towards electric mobility is also boosting the demand for electric vehicle polyurethane adhesives.

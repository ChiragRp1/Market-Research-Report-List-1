<p><ul><li>Roller Chain</li><li>Bush Chain</li><li>Toothed/Silent Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive metal timing chain market consists of three main types: roller chain, bush chain, and toothed/silent chain. Roller chain is widely used in various automotive applications due to its durability and high load-bearing capacity. Bush chain, on the other hand, is commonly used in smaller vehicles and motorcycles, offering a compact design and reliable performance. Lastly, toothed/silent chain is known for its quiet operation and high efficiency, making it suitable for advanced automotive engines. Each type of chain serves a specific purpose in the automotive industry, catering to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>

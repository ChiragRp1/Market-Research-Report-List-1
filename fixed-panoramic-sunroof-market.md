<p><p>The market for fixed panoramic sunroofs can be categorized into two types based on the type of glass used: laminated glass and tempered glass. Laminated glass is made by sandwiching a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) between two layers of glass, providing enhanced safety as it holds together when shattered. On the other hand, tempered glass is treated with thermal or chemical processes to increase its strength, making it more resistant to breakage. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to specific customer preferences in the fixed panoramic sunroof market.</p></p>

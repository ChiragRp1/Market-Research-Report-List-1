<p><p>Trace element fertilizers, also known as micronutrient fertilizers, are compounds that provide essential nutrients to plants in small quantities. Rising awareness about the importance of trace elements in crop nutrition and the increasing need for higher agricultural productivity are the primary drivers of market growth.

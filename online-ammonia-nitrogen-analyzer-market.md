<p><p>The market for online ammonia nitrogen analyzers can be classified into different types based on the technology used. One such type is Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE), which involves the use of electrodes that selectively respond to ammonia ions. Another type is Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance, where the analyzer measures the absorbance of UV light by ammonia molecules. Lastly, there is the Colorimetric market, which relies on the change in color intensity due to the reaction between ammonia and a specific reagent. These technologies enable accurate and efficient measurement of ammonia nitrogen levels in various industries and applications.</p></p>

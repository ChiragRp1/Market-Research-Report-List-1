<p><p>The nonwoven geotextiles market is categorized into three types: PP (Polypropylene), PET (Polyester), and PE (Polyethylene). PP geotextiles are made from synthetic fibers that provide excellent resistance to chemicals and UV light. PET geotextiles, also made from synthetic fibers, offer higher strength and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring high tensile strength. PE geotextiles are made from polyethylene and are known for their puncture resistance and flexibility. These materials are widely used in construction and civil engineering projects for erosion control, soil stabilization, drainage, and filtration applications.</p></p>

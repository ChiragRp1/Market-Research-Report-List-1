<p><p>Casement windows are a type of window that is hinged at the side and opens outward, similar to a door. Casement windows are considered energy-efficient as they provide a tight seal when closed, minimizing air leakage.</p><p>The casement windows market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years. This can be attributed to various factors, including the increasing demand for energy-efficient buildings and the growing preference for modern architectural designs.

<p><p>The Automotive Door Latch Actuator market can be classified into two types: DC Motor Type and Relay Type. The DC Motor Type actuator utilizes a direct current (DC) motor to drive the latch mechanism, providing a high degree of precision and control. On the other hand, the Relay Type actuator uses an electromagnetic relay to activate and control the latch mechanism. This type offers a simpler design and lower cost compared to the DC Motor Type actuator. Both types serve the purpose of opening and closing automotive doors.</p></p>

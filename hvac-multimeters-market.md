<p><p>The HVAC multimeters market includes two types: digital and analog. Digital multimeters use digital displays to provide readings, making them easy to read and precise. They offer various functions such as measuring voltage, current, resistance, and temperature. On the other hand, analog multimeters use a needle that moves across a scale to indicate readings. They are simpler in design, but can still provide accurate measurements. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in the HVAC industry for troubleshooting, maintenance, and installation of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.</p></p>

<p><p>Transplantation diagnostics market includes two main types - instruments and reagents. Instruments are essential devices utilized in various diagnostic procedures such as PCR (polymerase chain reaction), flow cytometry, and NGS (next-generation sequencing). These instruments aid in analyzing and detecting the compatibility between the donor and recipient tissues during transplantation. On the other hand, reagents are specific substances used alongside these instruments to support the diagnostic testing process, enabling accurate and reliable results. Both instruments and reagents play an indispensable role in the transplantation diagnostics market by ensuring successful organ transplantation procedures.</p></p>

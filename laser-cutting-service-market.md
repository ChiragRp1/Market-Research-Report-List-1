<p><p>The laser cutting service market can be categorized into three types: CO2 Laser Cutting, Fiber Laser Cutting, and Others. CO2 Laser Cutting utilizes gas lasers to produce high energy beams for cutting various materials like wood, acrylic, and fabric. Fiber Laser Cutting employs solid-state lasers for precise cutting of metals, including stainless steel and aluminum. The Others category includes alternative laser technologies like Nd:YAG and disk lasers. Each type offers unique advantages, such as different power levels and wavelength ranges, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>

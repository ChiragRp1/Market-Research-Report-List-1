<p><p>The automotive belt tensioner market encompasses two main types: manual tensioners and automatic tensioners. Manual tensioners require manual adjustment of belt tension using a mechanical device. They are simpler and more cost-effective but require regular maintenance. On the other hand, automatic tensioners use a spring-loaded mechanism to automatically adjust belt tension, ensuring optimal performance and reducing the need for regular maintenance. Although more expensive initially, automatic tensioners offer convenience and efficiency in the long run.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Automotive CO2 Heat Pump Air Conditioning Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct</li><li>Indirect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct market in the automotive CO2 heat pump air conditioning refers to the systems where the heat transfer occurs directly between the CO2 refrigerant and the passenger compartment of the vehicle. This type of system provides efficient cooling and heating for the occupants. </p><p>On the other hand, the indirect market involves a two-step process, where the CO2 refrigerant transfers the heat to a secondary medium (such as water or air) before heating or cooling the passenger compartment. These systems offer flexibility as they can be combined with other heating or cooling sources, but they may have slightly lower efficiency compared to the direct systems.</p></p>

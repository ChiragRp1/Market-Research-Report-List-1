<p><strong>The Thermoplastic Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The thermoplastic geomembrane market consists of various types such as HDPE, LLDPE, PVC, and others. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes offer excellent chemical resistance and are commonly used for lining applications in landfills and containment ponds. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) geomembranes have high flexibility and are suitable for applications that require greater elongation, such as waste containment and wastewater treatment. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) geomembranes offer superior resistance to UV radiation and are typically used in exposed applications like decorative ponds. Other types include EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) and CSPE (Chlorosulfonated Polyethylene) geomembranes, which offer unique properties for specific applications in the industry.</p></p>

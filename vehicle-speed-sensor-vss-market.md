<p><p>Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) is a crucial component in modern vehicles that measures the speed of the vehicle's transmission output shaft. There are three types of VSS available in the market. The Optical Vehicle Speed Sensor uses an infrared light beam to detect the speed. The Hall-type Vehicle Speed Sensor generates a voltage signal when the magnetic field changes. The Magnetic-electric Vehicle Speed Sensor uses a magnetic field to measure the speed. These sensors are essential for accurate speed readings and are widely used in automotive applications.</p></p>

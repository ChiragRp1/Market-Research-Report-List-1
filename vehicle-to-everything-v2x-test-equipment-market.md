DSRC (dedicated short-range communications) connectivity involves vehicle communication using a specific frequency band. It allows vehicles to directly communicate with each other and with roadside infrastructure for various applications. On the other hand, cellular connectivity involves vehicles using cellular networks for communication. This enables vehicles to connect to the internet and access various services. Both types offer different advantages and are important for testing and evaluating V2X communication systems.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Paste PVC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Type</li><li>Heterogeneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paste PVC resin market is divided into two types: homogeneous type and heterogeneous type. In homogeneous type, the PVC resin particles have a uniform size and distribution, resulting in a smooth, consistent paste. This type is preferred for applications that require a high level of transparency and clarity. On the other hand, heterogeneous type consists of PVC resin particles with different sizes, resulting in a paste with a more textured and uneven appearance. This type is commonly used for applications that prioritize surface texture and aesthetics.</p></p>

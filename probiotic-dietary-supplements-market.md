<p><p>Probiotic dietary supplements are categorized into three types: lactobacillus class, bifidobacteria, and gram-positive cocci. The lactobacillus class refers to a type of bacteria that is commonly found in fermented foods and yogurts. Bifidobacteria are another type of bacteria that can be found in the gut and are known for their beneficial effects on digestive health. Lastly, gram-positive cocci include bacteria species like Streptococcus and Staphylococcus, which can also provide health benefits when consumed as probiotic supplements. These different types offer various strains and benefits for consumers seeking probiotic supplementation.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Powder-Actuated Nail Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive Nail Gun</li><li>Indirect Drive Nail Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder-actuated nail gun market consists of two types: direct drive nail guns and indirect drive nail guns. Direct drive nail guns use a piston to directly drive a nail into the material, offering quick and precise installation. On the other hand, indirect drive nail guns use a moving chamber that strikes an intermediate object, driving the nail into the material. This type provides versatility and efficiency for various applications. Both types are widely used in construction and carpentry, allowing for efficient and secure fastening of materials.</p></p>

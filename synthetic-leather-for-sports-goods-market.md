Microfiber PU provides superior breathability, water resistance, and lightweight properties. Ecological function PU emphasizes environmental friendliness through the use of sustainable materials and production methods. These diverse market types cater to different consumer preferences, offering a wide range of options in the sports goods industry.</p></p>

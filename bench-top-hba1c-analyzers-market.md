<p><p>Bench-top HbA1c analyzers are medical devices used for measuring the levels of HbA1c (glycated hemoglobin) in blood samples. There are three main types of technology used in these analyzers. Ion exchange chromatography separates HbA1c based on its charge, while immunoassay utilizes specific antibodies that bind to HbA1c. Lastly, boronate affinity technology relies on the reversible interaction between boronic acid and HbA1c. Each of these technologies offers different advantages and limitations, providing healthcare professionals with diverse options for accurate HbA1c measurement.</p></p>

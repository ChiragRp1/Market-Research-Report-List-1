<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic PERC cells market consists of two main types: single-sided PERC cells and double-sided PERC cells. Single-sided PERC cells have a passivated rear surface and allow sunlight to pass through only one side of the cell. On the other hand, double-sided PERC cells have passivated rear and front surfaces, enabling sunlight to be absorbed from both sides. Single-sided PERC cells are commonly used in standard solar panels, while double-sided PERC cells are employed in bifacial solar panels that can generate electricity from both direct and reflected sunlight.</p></p>

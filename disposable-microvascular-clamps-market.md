<p><p>The disposable microvascular clamps market comprises two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. These clamps are preferred for shorter procedures or instances where long-term clamping is not required. These clamps are commonly used for longer procedures that require extended clamping, ensuring a secure hold on blood vessels during surgeries or other medical procedures.</p></p>

<p><p>Software Asset Management (SAM) Software Market Types include on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise SAM software is installed and managed on the user's servers or data centers. It requires local infrastructure, maintenance, and regular updates. In contrast, cloud-based SAM software is delivered through the internet and hosted on the provider's servers. It offers flexibility, scalability, and remote accessibility, eliminating the need for on-site resources and reducing maintenance efforts. Cloud-based SAM software also often provides real-time updates and automatic backups, ensuring data protection and enhanced collaboration.</p></p>

In air cooling, the excess heat generated by the drive is dissipated through air circulation, ensuring the system's temperature remains within safe limits. Conversely, water cooling manages the drive's temperature by circulating water, which absorbs and carries away the heat, providing efficient cooling for optimal performance. These cooling methods play a crucial role in maintaining the drive's operational efficiency and longevity.</p></p>

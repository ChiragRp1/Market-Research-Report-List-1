<p><p>Pneumatic suspension market types include capsule, membrane, and compound designs. Capsule suspension systems use compressed air in a single chamber for the entire suspension function, offering a simple and cost-effective solution. Membrane suspensions, on the other hand, utilize multiple chambers separated by flexible membranes to provide greater stability and adjustability. Compound suspension systems combine both capsule and membrane designs, providing a balance between simplicity and enhanced performance. These different types cater to various needs, offering options for different budgets and requirements in the pneumatic suspension market.</p></p>

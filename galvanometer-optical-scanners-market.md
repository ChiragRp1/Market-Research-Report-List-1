<p><p>Galvanometer optical scanners are widely used in various industries for precise and rapid scanning. Single-axis scanners operate on a single plane, allowing for scanning in one direction. They are often used in applications where scanning is required in only one dimension. Dual-axis scanners, on the other hand, are capable of scanning in two planes, enabling scanning in both horizontal and vertical directions. This makes them suitable for applications that require scanning in two dimensions or for creating more complex patterns. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in applications such as laser marking, laser engraving, and 3D printing.</p></p>

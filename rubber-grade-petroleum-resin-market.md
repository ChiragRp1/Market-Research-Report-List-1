<p><ul><li>C5 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C9 Petroleum Resin</li><li>C5/C9 Petroleum Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber grade petroleum resin market is comprised of three main types: C5 Petroleum Resin, C9 Petroleum Resin, and C5/C9 Petroleum Resin. C5 Petroleum Resin is derived from C5 fraction of cracked petroleum and is used as a tackifier in rubber products. C9 Petroleum Resin, on the other hand, is made from C9 fraction and finds application as a reinforcing agent in rubber. C5/C9 Petroleum Resin is a blend of both C5 and C9 resins, offering a combination of tackifying and reinforcing properties for rubber formulations.</p></p>

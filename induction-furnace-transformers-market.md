<p><p>Induction furnace transformers are used in the steel and foundry industries for efficient and precise heating processes. The two main types of induction furnace transformers are oil-filled transformers and dry type transformers. Oil-filled transformers use oil as an insulating medium and have a higher power capacity, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, dry type transformers use solid insulation materials, eliminating the risk of oil leakage and making them more environmentally friendly. However, they have lower power capacities and are generally used in lighter-duty applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The E-Compass market comprises various types of sensors that help determine accurate direction and positioning. Fluxgate sensors utilize a ferromagnetic core to measure changes in magnetic fields. Hall-effect sensors detect changes in voltage caused by the interaction of a current-carrying conductor and a magnetic field. Magneto Resistive sensors measure resistance changes when subjected to a magnetic field. Apart from these, there are other types of sensors available such as magnetometers and gyroscope-based sensors that contribute to the E-Compass market, providing reliable navigation and orientation capabilities.</p></p>

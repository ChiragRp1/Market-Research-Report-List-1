<p><p>The MLCC Inner Electrode Paste market consists of three main types: Ni Paste, Au Paste, and Pb Paste. Ni Paste refers to the use of nickel as the conductive material in the paste, providing high electrical conductivity and stability. Au Paste, on the other hand, utilizes gold as the conductive material, offering superior conductivity and resistance to oxidation. Pb Paste employs lead as the conductive material, which has good conductivity but poses environmental concerns due to its toxicity. These different types cater to different needs and requirements in the manufacturing of MLCC inner electrodes.</p></p>

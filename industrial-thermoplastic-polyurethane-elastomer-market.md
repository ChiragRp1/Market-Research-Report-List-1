<p><p>The industrial thermoplastic polyurethane elastomer market can be categorized into three types: polyester-based, polyether-based, and others. Polyester-based elastomers are known for their excellent resistance to chemicals, weathering, and abrasion, making them ideal for industrial applications. Polyether-based elastomers, on the other hand, have exceptional resistance to moisture, microorganisms, and hydrolysis, making them suitable for applications where exposure to water or humidity is expected. The "others" category includes various specialty elastomers that possess unique properties like flame retardancy, high heat resistance, or enhanced mechanical strength, catering to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>

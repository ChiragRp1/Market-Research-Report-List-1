<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The sealed garbage truck market consists of three main types: the seal cover type, slide-out cover type, and hydraulic clamshell type. The seal cover type is equipped with a durable cover that tightly encloses the garbage container, preventing any leaks or odors from escaping. The slide-out cover type features a retractable cover that glides horizontally, making it easier for garbage collectors to load the waste. Lastly, the hydraulic clamshell type uses a hydraulic system to open and close two hinged covers, allowing for efficient and quick disposal of garbage.</p></p>

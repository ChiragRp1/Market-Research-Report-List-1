<p><p>An infant nasal cannula is a medical device specifically designed for newborns and infants to deliver supplemental oxygen. This cannula allows for a continuous flow of oxygen, ensuring that the baby receives adequate oxygenation and improves their respiratory function.</p><p>The future outlook of the infant nasal cannula market is very promising. The market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecasted period, with a projected CAGR of 9.4%. Several factors contribute to this steady growth. Firstly, an increase in premature births and neonatal respiratory disorders is driving the demand for infant nasal cannulas. As preterm infants often have underdeveloped lungs, they require respiratory support to survive and thrive. Additionally, the rising prevalence of chronic lung diseases among infants, such as bronchopulmonary dysplasia, further fuels the demand for these devices.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in the design and manufacturing of infant nasal cannulas are expected to drive market growth. Manufacturers are investing in developing new materials and designs that provide greater comfort, reduce skin irritation, and improve overall safety for infants.

<p><ul><li>Digital Signal Processing Hearing Aids</li><li>Simulated Signal Processing Hearing Aids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ITE (in-the-ear) hearing aids market consists of two major types: Digital Signal Processing (DSP) and Simulated Signal Processing (SSP) hearing aids. DSP hearing aids use advanced technology to process sound signals digitally, resulting in improved sound quality and customization options. On the other hand, SSP hearing aids simulate the natural sound processing ability of the ear by amplifying certain frequencies, but lack the technological advancements and customization features of DSP hearing aids. Both types aim to enhance hearing abilities for individuals with hearing loss, albeit with varying levels of sophistication.</p></p>

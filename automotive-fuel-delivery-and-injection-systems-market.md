<p><p>Automotive fuel delivery and injection systems refer to the mechanisms responsible for delivering fuel into the engine cylinders of vehicles. There are different types of fuel injectors used in the market. Throttle body fuel injectors distribute fuel to the cylinders through a single point, while direct fuel injectors directly inject fuel into each cylinder. Sequential fuel injectors distribute fuel in a precise sequence to optimize engine performance, and port fuel injectors distribute fuel at the intake port of each cylinder. These different types cater to varying fuel delivery and injection needs in vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>Air hose market types can be broadly classified into two categories: Rubber Hose and PVC Hose. Rubber Hose is made from natural or synthetic rubber, offering excellent flexibility and durability. It is ideal for heavy-duty applications that require high pressure and temperature resistance. On the other hand, PVC Hose is made from polyvinyl chloride, providing good flexibility and affordability. It is commonly used in lighter applications, such as air compressors and pneumatic tools. Both rubber and PVC hoses have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Teak oil is available in two main market types: raw teak oil and mixed teak oil. Raw teak oil is derived directly from teak wood, with minimal processing or additives added. It maintains the original color and texture of the wood while providing a natural protective layer. On the other hand, mixed teak oil is a blend of raw teak oil with additional ingredients such as solvents or resins. This type is often used for commercial purposes as it enhances the durability and appearance of teak wood, ideal for furniture or outdoor applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The rock formation drilling rig market can be categorized into three main types: rotary, impact, and others. The rotary drilling rigs are widely used and involve the rotation of a drill bit to create a hole. Impact drilling rigs, on the other hand, use percussive action to break the rock formation. Lastly, the "others" category comprises various drilling methods like directional drilling, sonic drilling, and hydraulic drilling. Each type offers unique advantages and is used depending on the specific geological conditions and drilling requirements.</p></p>

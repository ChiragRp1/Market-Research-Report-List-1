<p><p>Thermal flow meters are widely used in industries to measure the flow rate of fluids. There are several types of thermal flow meters available in the market. Ultrasonic flow meters use sound waves to measure flow rates. Coriolis flow meters rely on the Coriolis effect to measure mass flow rates. Turbine flow meters use a rotating turbine to measure flow rates. Vortex flow meters measure flow rates based on the frequency of vortices formed. Magnetic flow meters use magnetic fields to measure flow rates. Differential pressure flow meters measure flow rates based on pressure differences. Positive displacement flow meters measure flow rates by dividing the fluid into discrete volumes.</p></p>

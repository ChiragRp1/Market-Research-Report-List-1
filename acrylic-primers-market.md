<p><p>Acrylic primers are used to prepare various surfaces for painting or applying other coatings. Water-based acrylic primers are diluted with water and provide a smooth and even base for painting. These primers are eco-friendly, quick-drying, and easy to clean up. On the other hand, non-water-based acrylic primers use solvents as their base, providing a strong bond to the surface and enhanced adhesion. However, they may take longer to dry and require proper ventilation during application. Both types offer effective priming solutions but cater to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>

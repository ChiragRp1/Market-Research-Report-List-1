Cross-linked PPS composite is created by chemically bonding the polymer chains, resulting in improved mechanical and thermal properties such as increased strength and stability. In contrast, linear PPS composite is created without cross-linking, retaining the original properties of PPS but with enhanced flexibility and processability. Each market type caters to different applications and industries based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>

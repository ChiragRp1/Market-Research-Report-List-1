<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing Equipment (NDTE) refers to equipment used to inspect and evaluate materials or components without causing damage. The Ultrasonic testing equipment utilizes ultrasound waves to detect flaws, while Magnetic particle testing equipment uses magnetic fields to identify imperfections. Visual inspection equipment involves directly observing the surface conditions. Radiography testing equipment uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect internal structures. Penetrant testing equipment detects surface cracks using colored dyes. Eddy current testing equipment applies electromagnetic induction to spot surface defects. Acoustic emission testing equipment detects abnormalities through sound waves.</p></p>

<p><p>Gellan gum is a widely used food additive and thickening agent. It comes in two market types: low acyl gellan gum and high acyl gellan gum. Low acyl gellan gum provides strong gelling properties and is often used in solid gels, desserts, and confectionery products. On the other hand, high acyl gellan gum offers excellent suspension, stabilization, and texture enhancement capabilities. It is commonly used in beverages, sauces, dressings, and dairy products. Both types of gellan gum play important roles in improving the texture, stability, and overall quality of various food and beverage products.</p></p>

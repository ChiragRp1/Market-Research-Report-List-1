<p><p>The HDPE geogrid market consists of two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to provide strength and stability in multiple directions, making them ideal for applications such as soil reinforcement and slope stabilization. On the other hand, uniaxial tension geogrids are specifically engineered to provide strength in one direction, primarily used for load support and pavement reinforcement. Both types of geogrids offer unique benefits and are widely used in various civil engineering and construction projects.</p></p>

<p><p>Human Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin (HBIG) is a type of immunoglobulin derived from the plasma of individuals who have high levels of antibodies against the hepatitis B virus (HBV). HBIG works by providing passive immunization to individuals at risk of HBV infection, especially infants born to mothers who are HBV positive.</p><p>The Human Hepatitis B Immunoglobulin (HBIG) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period. Several factors are driving the growth of this market. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of Hepatitis B globally is creating a greater need for HBIG as a preventive measure. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), approximately 257 million people are chronically infected with HBV, leading to several associated complications such as liver cirrhosis and hepatocellular carcinoma.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness about HBV infection and the importance of vaccination in preventing its transmission is also contributing to the demand for HBIG.

<p><p>The New Energy Buses market is divided into two main types: Battery Electric Buses (BEB) and Hybrid Buses. Battery Electric Buses are powered solely by electricity, with large rechargeable batteries providing the energy needed for propulsion. They produce zero emissions, reducing the environmental impact of public transportation. Hybrid Buses, on the other hand, combine both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine. The electric motor assists the engine, resulting in reduced fuel consumption and emissions compared to traditional diesel buses.</p></p>

<p><p>The flexible epoxy adhesive market consists of two types: one-component adhesive and two-component adhesive. One-component adhesive is premixed and requires no additional mixing or preparation before use. It provides convenience and ease of application. Two-component adhesive, on the other hand, consists of two separate components that need to be mixed before use. This type of adhesive offers improved strength and versatility as the curing process only begins when the components are mixed together. Both types provide flexibility, bonding strength, and durability, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>

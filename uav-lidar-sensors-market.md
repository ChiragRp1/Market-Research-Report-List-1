<p><p>UAV lidar sensors are used in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for mapping, surveying, and other remote sensing applications. Solid-state lidar sensors use solid materials, such as semiconductors, to emit and receive laser pulses, offering compactness and reliability. Mechanical lidar sensors, on the other hand, use rotating mirrors or oscillating prism to steer the laser beam, providing a larger field of view. Both types offer advantages and are suited for different applications, depending on factors such as cost, range, accuracy, and size requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Orthopedic braces and support devices are medical orthotic devices that are used to provide support, stability, and alignment to various parts of the body, primarily the musculoskeletal system. These devices are integrated with sensors, actuators, and connectivity features, allowing continuous monitoring of the patient's condition, real-time data transmission to healthcare professionals, and remote adjustments of the device. Smart braces not only provide support and immobilization but also enable personalized rehabilitation programs.</p><p>Moreover, the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries and the growing focus on preventive care are also driving the market. Patients and healthcare professionals are increasingly opting for braces and support devices as a non-surgical alternative or as a post-operative aid, promoting faster recovery.</p><p>Overall, the orthopedic braces and support devices market is poised for significant growth in the coming years, driven by technological advancements, increasing prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders, and the growing demand for personalized and non-invasive treatment options.</p></p>

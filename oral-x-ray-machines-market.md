There are two main types of oral X-ray machines on the market: analog X-ray and digital X-ray. Analog X-ray machines use traditional film, which is exposed to X-rays and then developed using chemical processes. Digital X-ray machines capture images using electronic sensors and display the results on a computer screen. Digital X-ray machines provide quick results, lower radiation exposure, and easier storage and sharing of images compared to analog X-ray machines.</p></p>

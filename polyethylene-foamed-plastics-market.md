<p><p>The Polyethylene Foamed Plastics Market can be categorized into three types based on manufacturing methods: mechanical, physical, and chemical methods. </p><p>Mechanical methods involve foaming the plastic by applying external forces such as compression or shearing. Physical methods utilize heat or steam to create and expand bubbles in the plastic. Lastly, chemical methods involve adding chemical blowing agents to trigger a controlled release of gases, resulting in foam formation. These methods play a crucial role in determining the final properties and characteristics of polyethylene foamed plastics used in various industries.</p></p>

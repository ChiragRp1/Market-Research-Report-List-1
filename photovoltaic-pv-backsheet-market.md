<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) backsheets are protective films used in solar panels. The fluoropolymer PV backsheet market includes backsheets made from materials like fluoropolymer, which offer excellent protection against UV radiation, moisture, and harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, the non-fluoropolymer PV backsheet market comprises backsheets made from materials like polyester or polyamide. While non-fluoropolymer backsheets may have lower UV resistance, they are usually more cost-effective. Both types serve the purpose of protecting the solar cells from external factors, ensuring the longevity and efficiency of the solar panels.</p></p>

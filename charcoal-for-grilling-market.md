Hardwood briquettes are made from compressed hardwood sawdust and provide consistent heat. Charcoal briquettes are made from a mixture of charcoal dust and a binding agent, resulting in easy ignition and long burn. Lump charcoal consists of natural hardwood chunks, offering intense heat and smoky flavor. Other types may include bamboo charcoal or compressed wood pellets.</p></p>

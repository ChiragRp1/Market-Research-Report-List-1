<p><strong>The Toner Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Non-Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toner resins are materials used in the production of toner cartridges for printing. They are categorized into two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic toner resins contain magnetic particles that help in the printing process by adhering to the drum using magnetic forces. On the other hand, non-magnetic toner resins do not contain magnetic particles and rely on electrostatic attraction to adhere to the drum. Both types play a crucial role in the quality and efficiency of printing, with magnetic toner resins being preferred for their better printing performance.</p></p>

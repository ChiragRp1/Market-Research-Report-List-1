<p><p>The water-based marker market can be classified into two types: fiber type and foaming type. Fiber-type markers use a fine tip made of small fibers to transfer ink onto surfaces. They are commonly used for precise and detailed work. On the other hand, foaming-type markers have a broad tip that produces a foam-like texture when applied to surfaces. These markers are perfect for creating bold and vibrant lines or covering larger areas with color. Both marker types rely on water-based ink, making them easy to blend and clean up.</p></p>

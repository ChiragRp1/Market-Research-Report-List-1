Image Guided X-ray Irradiators provide real-time imaging and accurate radiation delivery, making them suitable for preclinical research and small animal testing. On the other hand, Cabinet X-ray Irradiators are compact and enclosed systems commonly used in industrial and medical settings for sterilization, material testing, and blood irradiation.

There are two types of bladder coatings in the market: permanent and semi-permanent. Permanent coatings form a strong bond with the bladder, offering long-lasting protection against sticking. Semi-permanent coatings provide a temporary barrier that can be easily removed or reapplied. Both types of coatings enhance the efficiency of the tire manufacturing process and ensure high-quality end products.</p></p>

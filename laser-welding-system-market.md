<p><p>The laser welding system market includes various types of machines, such as solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines use laser beams generated through solid materials like crystals, fiber, or glass. They provide high precision and are commonly used in industries like automotive, electronics, and medical. On the other hand, CO2 laser welding machines use carbon dioxide gas as the laser medium. They are ideal for welding materials like plastics, wood, textiles, and metals with high reflective surfaces.</p></p>

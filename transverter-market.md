<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transverter market consists of two main types: open loop and closed loop systems. Open loop transverters operate without feedback control, meaning they do not continuously adjust their output based on external conditions or signals. Closed loop transverters, on the other hand, use a feedback mechanism to regulate their output and respond to changes in the environment or input signals. Open loop systems are generally simpler and less expensive, but closed loop systems offer more precise and stable performance.</p></p>

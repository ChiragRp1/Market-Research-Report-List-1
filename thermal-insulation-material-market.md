<p><p>The thermal insulation material market consists of various types of materials including glass wool, stone wool, PU foam, polyisocyanurate, and others. PU foam, on the other hand, is a synthetic material known for its high thermal insulation properties. Polyisocyanurate is a type of rigid foam insulation, which provides superior thermal insulation and is commonly used in construction. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various materials such as mineral wool, cellulose, and aerogel.</p></p>

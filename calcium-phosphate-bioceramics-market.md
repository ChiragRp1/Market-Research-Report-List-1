Tricalcium phosphate is highly absorbable and stimulates bone regeneration. Monocalcium phosphate monohydrate assists in bone healing. Dicalcium phosphate is used in dental fillings. Other types may include combinations or variations of these materials. The calcium phosphate bioceramics market offers a wide range of options to suit different medical needs.</p></p>

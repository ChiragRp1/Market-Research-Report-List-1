<p><ul><li>Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels</li><li>Non Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oxygen indicator labels are used to provide visual indication of the oxygen levels in a packaging or storage environment. Reversible oxygen indicator labels change color based on the oxygen concentration and can be reused multiple times. Non-reversible oxygen indicator labels, on the other hand, permanently change color when exposed to certain levels of oxygen and cannot be reused. Both types of labels serve the purpose of alerting users to the presence or absence of oxygen, but differ in their reusability.</p></p>

<p><p>Super-capacitors and ultra-capacitors are energy storage devices used to power electronic devices. The market types include double layer capacitor, pseudo-capacitor, and hybrid capacitor. Double layer capacitors store energy in an electric field and are typically used in high-power applications. Pseudo-capacitors store energy through a surface redox reaction and are suited for medium-power applications. Hybrid capacitors combine the best features of both types and are suitable for a wide range of applications, offering high energy density and power density.</p></p>

<p><p>The offshore support vessel market can be categorized into two types: shallow type and deepwater type. These vessels are suitable for supporting activities such as oil and gas exploration, maintenance, and transportation in relatively shallow waters. On the other hand, deepwater type vessels are specifically built for offshore operations in water depths exceeding 200 meters. These vessels are equipped with advanced technologies and capabilities to support deepwater drilling, subsea construction, and installation activities in challenging deep-sea environments.</p></p>

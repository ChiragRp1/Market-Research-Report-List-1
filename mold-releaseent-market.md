<p><p>The Mold Releaseent market is categorized into three types: water-based, oily-based, and other. Water-based mold release agents are environmentally friendly and offer easy application and cleanup. They are commonly used in industries requiring low toxicity and minimal impact on product quality. Oily-based mold release agents contain a higher amount of lubricating oil, ensuring excellent mold separation but requiring more time for solvent evaporation. The "other" category includes specialty mold release agents specifically designed for unique applications, such as high-temperature molds or complex shapes.</p></p>

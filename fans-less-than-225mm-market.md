<p><p>The market for fans with a diameter less than 225mm can be categorized into two main types: axial fans and centrifugal fans. Axial fans are designed to move air parallel to the fan's axis, making them suitable for applications requiring high airflow volumes at low pressures. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, move air radially, generating higher pressures than axial fans, making them ideal for applications requiring increased resistance or pressure buildup. These market types cater to various industries such as HVAC, automotive, electronics, and appliances.</p></p>

Collagen-coated microcarriers have a thin layer of collagen on their surface, enhancing cell attachment. Other types of microcarriers may include fibronectin-coated, gelatin-coated, or hyaluronic acid-coated beads, offering specific advantages in different cell culture applications. These variations in microcarrier types provide researchers with options to optimize their cell culture conditions for enhanced growth, proliferation, and productivity.</p></p>

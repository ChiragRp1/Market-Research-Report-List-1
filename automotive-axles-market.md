<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive axles market includes various types of axles based on their design and functionality. Half-floating axles, also known as semi-floating axles, support the weight of the vehicle and transmit power to the wheels, but the axle shaft is a load-bearing component. Three-quarter floating axles have a load-bearing axle shaft and a separate hub assembly, making them stronger than semi-floating axles. Fully floating rear axles are the strongest, with both axle shaft and hub assembly being independent, allowing them to bear heavy loads without affecting the vehicle's drivetrain.</p></p>

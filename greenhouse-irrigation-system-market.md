<p><strong>The Greenhouse Irrigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drip irrigation systems</li><li>Sprinkler irrigation systems</li><li>Boom irrigation systems</li><li>Capillary irrigation system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Greenhouse irrigation systems refer to the methods used to provide water to plants in a controlled environment. Drip irrigation systems involve delivering small, precise amounts of water directly to the plant's root zone. Sprinkler irrigation systems distribute water through overhead sprinklers, mimicking natural rainfall. Boom irrigation systems have a moving boom with nozzles that spray water onto plants. Capillary irrigation systems use capillary action to draw water through a mat or wick into the plant's root zone. These systems ensure efficient water usage, leading to better plant growth and higher yield in greenhouse cultivation.</p></p>

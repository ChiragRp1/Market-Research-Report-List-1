<p><p>The carbon fiber filament market encompasses two major types: injection molding and extrusion molding. Injection molding involves heating the raw carbon fiber material and injecting it into a mold at high pressure to shape it into desired products. This process is commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics. On the other hand, extrusion molding involves forcing the molten carbon fiber material through a die to create continuous shapes such as tubes, rods, and sheets. This technique finds applications in industries like construction, consumer goods, and renewable energy.</p></p>

<p><p>The Medium Voltage Motor Protectors Market includes different types of protection relays. Feeder Protection Relays are used to safeguard the electrical distribution systems by detecting and isolating faults such as short circuits and overloads. They ensure continuous power supply and protect equipment from damage. Differential Protection Relays, on the other hand, monitor the difference in current between the winding phases of a motor to detect internal faults. These relays prevent motor damage by rapidly disconnecting it when an abnormality is detected, ensuring the motor's safe operation.</p></p>

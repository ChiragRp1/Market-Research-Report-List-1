<p><p>The architectural paints and coatings market can be categorized into three major types: water-borne, solvent-borne, and other coatings. Water-borne paints and coatings are made using water as a solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-borne coatings, on the other hand, use organic solvents as a base, allowing for better adhesion and durability. The "other" category encompasses various types such as powder coatings, radiation-cured coatings, and specialty coatings, each with unique properties and applications. These diverse market types cater to different needs and preferences in the architectural coatings industry.</p></p>

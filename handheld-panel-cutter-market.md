<p><p>The handheld panel cutter market consists of two main types: cordless and wired. Cordless panel cutters are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering portability and flexibility. They eliminate the need for electrical outlets, making them suitable for outdoor use or in situations where power access is limited. On the other hand, wired panel cutters rely on a power cord connected to an electrical outlet for operation. This ensures a constant power supply, making them ideal for continuous usage or projects that require high levels of cutting precision.</p></p>

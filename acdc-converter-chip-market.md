<p><p>AC/DC converter chip market comprises two main types: linear AC/DC converters and Switch-Mode Power Supply (SMPS). Linear AC/DC converters use linear regulators to convert AC power to DC, providing stable output voltage but are less efficient and generate more heat. On the other hand, SMPS uses high-frequency switching to convert AC power to DC, offering higher efficiency and compactness but may produce more electrical noise. These converter chips play a crucial role in various electronic devices, ranging from small battery chargers to industrial power supplies.</p></p>

<p><p>High-powered blenders are available in two market types: immersion type and non-immersion type. Immersion blenders are handheld devices that can be immersed directly into a container to blend ingredients. They are compact, easy to use, and perfect for making soups, sauces, and smoothies. On the other hand, non-immersion blenders are countertop appliances with a container and a fixed blending blade. They are powerful and suitable for tasks like crushing ice, making nut butter, and blending large batches. Each market type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>

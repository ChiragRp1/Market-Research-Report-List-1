The Onestep Method involves the direct reaction between fatty alcohol and glucose in the presence of an acid catalyst. The Twostep Method involves the formation of an alkyl glucoside followed by its conversion into APG. The Other Method includes alternative approaches for APG production, such as enzymatic or microwave-assisted methods.</p></p>

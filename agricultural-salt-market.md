<p><p>The agricultural salt market is composed of three main types: sea salt, well and rock salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is obtained by evaporating seawater, and it is rich in minerals. Well and rock salt are extracted from underground deposits through mining processes. They contain fewer impurities compared to sea salt. Lake salt is obtained by evaporating saltwater lakes, and it is also known for its purity. These different types of salt cater to the diverse agricultural needs of farmers and are used for various purposes such as fertilizers, livestock feed, and soil conditioning.</p></p>

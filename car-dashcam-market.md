<p><p>The car dashcam market consists of two main types: single channel dashcams and multi-channel dashcams. Single channel dashcams are equipped with a single camera that is mounted on the windshield of a vehicle and records the front view. These dashcams are popular among individual vehicle owners as they provide evidence in case of accidents or incidents. On the other hand, multi-channel dashcams come with multiple cameras that can be installed to record views from different angles such as the front, rear, or sides of the vehicle. These dashcams are commonly used by commercial vehicle owners, such as taxi or delivery services, to monitor the surroundings of their vehicles for safety and security purposes.</p></p>

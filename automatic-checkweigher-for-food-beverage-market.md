<p><p>The food and beverage industry utilizes two main types of automatic checkweighers: in-motion checkweighers and intermittent checkweighers. In-motion checkweighers are designed to accurately weigh products while they are in motion, allowing for efficient and continuous monitoring. On the other hand, intermittent checkweighers are used for stationary or slower production lines, weighing products in batches. Both types of checkweighers are vital in ensuring the quality and compliance of food and beverage products, preventing underfilled or overweight items from reaching consumers.</p></p>

<p><p>Treadmills are fitness equipment used for exercising by walking, jogging, or running at controllable speeds. There are two main types of treadmills: manual and motorized treadmills. Manual treadmills require the user's physical effort to power the belt, making them a more affordable option. On the other hand, motorized treadmills have a built-in motor that moves the belt automatically, offering more features like adjustable speeds and incline settings. Motorized treadmills are generally more expensive due to their advanced functionalities, while manual treadmills offer a simpler and cost-effective option for basic workouts.</p></p>

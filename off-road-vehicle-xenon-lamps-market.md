<p><p>Off-Road Vehicle Xenon Lamps market offers two types: Built-In Igniter and External Igniter. Built-In Igniter lamps have an integrated igniter component, which means the igniter is built into the lamp itself. This design ensures a compact size and easy installation, making it suitable for various off-road applications. On the other hand, External Igniter lamps require a separate igniter unit to be connected externally to the lamp. This allows for flexibility in mounting and replacement, but may require additional wiring and mounting considerations.</p></p>

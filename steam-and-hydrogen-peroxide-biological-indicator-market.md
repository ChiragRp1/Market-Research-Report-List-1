<p><p>Steam and hydrogen peroxide biological indicators are used in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and healthcare industries to ensure the effectiveness of sterilization processes. The growth or absence of these spores indicates the efficacy of the sterilization process.</p><p>The market analysis of the steam and hydrogen peroxide biological indicator market shows promising growth. Factors such as the increasing demand for sterilization equipment in the healthcare industry, rising awareness about infection control, and stringent regulations for sterilization processes are driving the market growth. The introduction of technologically advanced biological indicators, such as self-contained biological indicators and rapid readout systems, is gaining traction in the market. These advancements offer improved accuracy, efficiency, and convenience in biological indicator testing.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the adoption of hydrogen peroxide biological indicators over steam indicators due to their compatibility with low-temperature sterilization processes.

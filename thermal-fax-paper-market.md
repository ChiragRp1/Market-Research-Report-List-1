<p><p>Thermal fax paper market encompasses various types such as top coating and non-top coating thermal paper, standard and premium thermal paper, and others. Top coating thermal paper has a specialized layer on its surface that enhances print quality and durability. Non-top coating thermal paper lacks this additional layer but is still commonly used due to its cost-effectiveness. Standard thermal paper is widely used for everyday faxing needs, while premium thermal paper offers higher durability and image resolution. Lastly, the "others" segment includes specialty thermal papers like colored or laminated papers catering to specific printing requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Functional fiber refers to a type of dietary fiber that provides various health benefits beyond basic nutrition. This trend is driving manufacturers to develop innovative and natural functional fiber ingredients to cater to consumer preferences.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the incorporation of functional fibers in confectionery and bakery products. <p><p>Functional fiber market consists of various types such as physical function, chemical function, material separability function, and bioadaptive function. Physical function refers to fibers that possess certain physical properties like high strength or elasticity. Chemical function refers to fibers that exhibit specific chemical properties like flame resistance or anti-bacterial properties. Material separability function refers to fibers that can be easily separated from other materials for recycling purposes. Lastly, bioadaptive function refers to fibers that can adapt to biological environments and exhibit properties like biodegradability or compatibility with human tissues.</p></p>

<p><p>A coal-fired boiler is a type of boiler that burns coal as its primary fuel source. It is widely used in industries that require steam for various operations, such as power generation, heating, and industrial processes. These boilers work by converting the chemical energy stored in coal into heat energy, which is then used to generate steam. The steam produced by the coal-fired boiler can be used for various applications, including electricity generation through steam turbines or heating processes in manufacturing plants.</p><p>The future outlook for the coal-fired boiler market is mixed, as the shift towards cleaner energy sources and increased environmental regulations pose challenges for this sector. However, the increasing focus on reducing greenhouse gas emissions and transitioning to cleaner energy sources has led to a decline in new coal-fired boiler installations in some parts of the world.</p><p>Despite these challenges, the coal-fired boiler market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecasted period. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the development of high-efficiency boilers and the use of clean coal technologies, are expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>In conclusion, while the coal-fired boiler market faces challenges due to environmental concerns, it continues to play a significant role in providing heat and power for industrial applications. <p><p>The coal-fired boiler market consists of different types of boilers. Circulating fluidized bed (CFB) boiler, SZL Series Boiler, DZL Series Boiler, SHL Series Boiler, SHH Series Boiler, and pulverized coal tower type boiler are some of the market types. CFB boilers use a fluidized bed for combustion, offering high fuel flexibility. SZL and DZL Series boilers are traditional types with a fixed grate that burn coal efficiently. SHL and SHH Series boilers have higher thermal efficiencies and reduced emissions. Pulverized coal tower type boilers are large-scale units that crush coal into fine powder before combustion.</p></p>

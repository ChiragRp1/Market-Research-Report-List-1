<p><p>Gradient thermal cyclers are used in molecular biology laboratories for amplifying DNA samples through a process called polymerase chain reaction (PCR). Block type gradient thermal cyclers have a metal block where the PCR tubes are inserted, ensuring uniform heating and cooling. Tube type gradient thermal cyclers, on the other hand, have separate individual wells for PCR tubes. Other types include robotic thermal cyclers and plate type thermal cyclers. These different market types offer scientists various options to choose from based on their specific experimental needs, sample volume, and throughput requirements.</p></p>

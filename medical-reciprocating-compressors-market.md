<p><p>Medical reciprocating compressors are used in various medical applications to generate compressed air. There are two types of reciprocating compressors: lubricated and oil-less. Lubricated compressors use oil to lubricate the piston and cylinder, which helps reduce friction and extend the lifespan of the compressor. On the other hand, oil-less compressors eliminate the need for oil, making them suitable for medical environments where oil contamination must be avoided. Both types of compressors play a crucial role in delivering reliable and clean compressed air for medical applications.</p></p>

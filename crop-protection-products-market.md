<p><p>Crop protection products can be classified into three main types - herbicides, insecticides, and fungicides. Herbicides are chemicals used to control or kill weeds that compete with crops for resources. Insecticides are substances that are employed to combat insects and other pest organisms that damage crops. Fungicides, on the other hand, are chemicals that prevent or eliminate fungal diseases that can affect crop health and productivity. These three types of crop protection products play a crucial role in safeguarding the growth and development of crops, ensuring higher agricultural yields.</p></p>

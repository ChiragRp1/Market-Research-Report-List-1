<p><p>The industrial adhesives and tapes market encompasses various types of adhesives and tapes catering to different applications. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and liquefy when heated, providing fast bonding. Contact spray adhesives are suitable for bonding large surfaces and offer strong initial tack. Acrylic adhesives provide excellent durability and flexibility, commonly used in automotive and construction industries. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, ideal for sealing and threadlocking applications. Epoxy adhesives offer high strength and chemical resistance. Additionally, there are other types of adhesives and tapes, each suited for specific industrial needs.</p></p>

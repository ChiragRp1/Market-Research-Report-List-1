<p><p>Super Swamper Tires are available in two main market types: bias tire and radial tire. Bias tires are constructed with crisscross ply layers, where the layers at an angle to the tread. They offer exceptional traction in off-road conditions due to their flexible sidewalls. On the other hand, radial tires have layers of cord plies running perpendicular to the tread, providing smoother rides and greater fuel efficiency on paved roads. These two types cater to distinct consumer needs, with bias tires preferred for off-roading and radial tires suited for everyday driving.</p></p>

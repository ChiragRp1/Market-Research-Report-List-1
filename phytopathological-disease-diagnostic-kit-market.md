<p><p>Phytopathological Disease Diagnostic Kits are used to identify and diagnose diseases affecting plants. There are two main types of kits available in the market: serological tests and molecular tests. Serological tests detect specific antibodies or antigens present in plant samples. They are cost-effective and user-friendly but may lack sensitivity and specificity. On the other hand, molecular tests identify genetic material like DNA or RNA to diagnose diseases. They offer high accuracy and sensitivity but may require specialized equipment and expertise. Both types play a crucial role in timely disease diagnosis and effective plant disease management.</p></p>

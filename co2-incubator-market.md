<p><p>CO2 incubators are specialized equipment used in scientific research and biotechnology industries to create a controlled environment for cell and tissue culture. There are two main types of CO2 incubator markets: heating and refrigeration. Heating CO2 incubators maintain a constant temperature necessary for cell growth and reproduction, typically between 37 to 40 degrees Celsius. Refrigeration CO2 incubators not only control the temperature but also chill the air to lower temperatures, allowing researchers to work with temperature-sensitive samples. Each type serves specific purposes in maintaining optimal conditions for cellular studies.</p></p>

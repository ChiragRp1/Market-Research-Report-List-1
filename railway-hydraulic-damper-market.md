<p><p>The railway hydraulic damper market is divided into two types: primary and secondary suspension dampers. Primary suspension dampers are used to support the weight of the train and provide a comfortable ride for passengers. Secondary suspension dampers are responsible for controlling the movement of the train in both horizontal and vertical directions, minimizing vibrations and improving stability. In addition, there is also a market for yaw dampers, which help in reducing the yawing motion of the train, preventing any potential derailment.</p></p>

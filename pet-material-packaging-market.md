<p><p>PET (Polyethylene terephthalate) material packaging is divided into two types: rigid packaging and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging refers to PET containers or bottles that have a fixed shape and structure, providing a sturdy and durable packaging solution. On the other hand, flexible packaging includes PET films or pouches that can be easily molded or shaped, allowing for versatile packaging options. Both types of PET material packaging offer unique advantages and can be used in various industries such as food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products.</p></p>

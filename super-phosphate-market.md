<p><p>Super Phosphate is a type of fertilizer that contains high amounts of phosphorous, an essential nutrient for plant growth. There are two main types of Super Phosphate in the market - Single Super Phosphate (SSP) and Triple Super Phosphate (TSP). SSP is made by treating rock phosphate with sulfuric acid, resulting in a fertilizer with 16-20% phosphorous content. On the other hand, TSP is produced by treating rock phosphate with phosphoric acid, creating a fertilizer with a phosphorous content of around 46-48%. Both SSP and TSP are widely used to enhance crop productivity and improve soil fertility.</p></p>

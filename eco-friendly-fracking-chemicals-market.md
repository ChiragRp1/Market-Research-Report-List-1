<p><ul><li>Gelling Agent</li><li>Crosslinker</li><li>Surfactant</li><li>Friction Reducer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for eco-friendly fracking chemicals can be classified into various types based on their functions. Gelling agents are substances that help to thicken the fracking fluid, allowing it to carry proppants and increase fracture width. Crosslinkers are chemicals that enhance the stability and viscosity of the fluid, ensuring better proppant transport. Surfactants reduce the surface tension of the fluid, enhancing its ability to mix with other substances. Friction reducers reduce fluid friction during the fracking process, increasing pump efficiency. Other types of chemicals may include pH adjusters, antiscalants, and biocides.</p></p>

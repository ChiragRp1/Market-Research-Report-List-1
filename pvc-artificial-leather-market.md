<p><p>PVC artificial leather is a type of synthetic leather made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) materials. It is commonly used as a substitute for genuine leather due to its durability and cost-effectiveness. The PVC artificial leather market includes two main types: calender PVC leather and rotary screen coating PVC artificial leather. Calender PVC leather is produced through a process where a calender machine applies pressure to the PVC material, resulting in a smooth and malleable leather-like finish. On the other hand, rotary screen coating PVC artificial leather is manufactured by coating PVC material onto a rotating screen, creating a textured and patterned leather surface. Both types serve various applications, such as automotive upholstery, furniture, bags, shoes, and clothing.</p></p>

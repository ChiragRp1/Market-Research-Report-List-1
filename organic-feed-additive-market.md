<p><p>Organic feed additives are natural and sustainable substances that are added to animal diets to enhance their overall health, performance, and productivity. Consumers are becoming more conscious about the source and quality of animal-based products, driving the need for natural and organic feed additives. Additionally, stringent regulations on the use of synthetic additives in animal feed have further boosted the adoption of organic alternatives.</p><p>The organic feed additive market is also benefitting from the rising awareness about animal welfare and environmental sustainability. Organic farming practices emphasize the use of sustainable and eco-friendly methods, and organic feed additives align with these principles by promoting animal health without causing adverse effects on the environment.</p><p>Probiotics and prebiotics have been gaining traction in the organic feed additive market due to their ability to improve digestion, immunity, and overall gut health in animals.

<p><p>Household kitchen blenders are categorized into two main types - countertop blenders and immersion blenders. Countertop blenders are the more traditional type and are designed with a pitcher-like container attached to a base with blades, allowing for versatile blending of ingredients. They are commonly used for making smoothies, soups, and other liquid-based recipes. On the other hand, immersion blenders, also known as hand blenders, are handheld devices with rotating blades at the end. They are immersed directly into a container or pot to blend ingredients, offering convenience and ease in smaller-scale blending tasks.</p></p>

<p><p>The anti-drone system market is categorized into three types: laser systems, kinetic systems, and electronic systems. Laser systems use high-powered lasers to disable or destroy drones by heating up their components. Kinetic systems rely on physical mechanisms such as nets, projectiles, or missiles to intercept and neutralize drones. Electronic systems employ radio frequency signals or jamming techniques to disrupt the communication between the drone and its operator, forcing it to land or return to its starting point. These different types offer varying approaches for countering unwanted drone activity.</p></p>

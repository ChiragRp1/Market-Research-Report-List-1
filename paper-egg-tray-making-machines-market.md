<p><p>Paper egg tray making machines are available in different market types, namely automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully computerized and require minimal human intervention, as they perform all operations from pulp molding to drying and stacking trays automatically. These machines are suitable for large-scale production and offer high efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of manual labor and supervision. While the pulp molding and drying processes are automated, tray stacking and packing need to be done manually. These machines are suitable for small to medium-scale production.</p></p>

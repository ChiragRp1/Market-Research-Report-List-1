<p><ul><li>Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Pneumatic Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roll briquetting machines are used to compress dry powdery materials into compact and convenient briquettes for easy transportation and storage. The mechanical transmission market involves using gears or belts to transmit power from the motor to the pressing roller, which controls the pressure applied to the materials. Hydraulic transmission market utilizes hydraulic systems to generate and control the pressure exerted on the materials. Pneumatic transmission market relies on compressed air to control the pressure. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities to meet the varying needs of users.</p></p>

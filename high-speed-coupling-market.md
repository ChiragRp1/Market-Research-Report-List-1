<p><p>The high-speed coupling market consists of different types of couplings used in various industries. Flexible elastic couplings absorb vibration and misalignment, preventing damage to connected equipment. Torsionally-rigid flexible couplings provide efficient torque transmission while allowing angular misalignment. Rigid couplings connect shafts directly, offering precise alignment but lack flexibility. Flange couplings are used to connect shafts with flanges, providing both angular misalignment and ease of installation. These couplings serve distinct purposes and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>

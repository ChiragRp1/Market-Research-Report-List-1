Honeycomb sandwich panels help in reducing the overall weight of a structure while maintaining its strength, making them a preferred choice in the aerospace and automotive industries.</p><p>Rapid industrialization and urbanization, coupled with growing infrastructure projects, are also expected to drive the demand for honeycomb sandwich panels in the construction industry.

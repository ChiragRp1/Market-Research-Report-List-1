<p><p>Bicycle motors can be classified into two primary market types: hub motor and crank motor. Hub motors are integrated into the wheel hub, providing direct power and propulsion to the wheel. They are known for their simplicity and ease of installation. On the other hand, crank motors are connected to the bike's crankshaft and provide power assistance through the pedals, making them a popular choice for electric bicycles. Both hub and crank motors offer different benefits and suit different riding preferences, allowing cyclists to choose the type that best fits their needs.</p></p>

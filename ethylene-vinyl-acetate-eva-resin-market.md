Tubular EVA is produced through a tubular reactor, resulting in high-density EVA with excellent transparency and toughness. Autoclave EVA is manufactured using an autoclave reactor, leading to medium to low-density EVA with outstanding processability and flexibility. Other process markets include the production of EVA through various processes like slurry, solution, and hybrid methods, offering different properties and applications.</p></p>

The market for NDT is divided into different types such as Magnetic Particle Tester, Ultrasonic Tester, Liquid Penetrant Tester, and Others. A Magnetic Particle Tester uses magnetic fields to identify defects like cracks and discontinuities. An Ultrasonic Tester employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws. A Liquid Penetrant Tester uses a liquid dye that seeps into surface cracks, making them visible. The Others segment includes various technologies like eddy current, radiography, and visual inspection to identify faults.</p></p>

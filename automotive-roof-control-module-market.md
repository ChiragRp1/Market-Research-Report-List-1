<p><p>The Automotive Roof Control Module (RCM) is a module primarily used in vehicles with convertible roofs or sunroofs. The RCM is an integrated system that consists of sensors, controllers, and electric motors, ensuring smooth operations of the roof system. Manufacturers are focusing on developing intelligent roof control modules that offer improved functionalities, such as automatic rain detection, anti-pinch safety features, and remote accessibility.

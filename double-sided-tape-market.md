<p><p>Double-sided tape, also known as double-coated tape or double-faced tape, is a type of adhesive tape that is coated with adhesive on both sides. It is used for bonding two surfaces together, where both sides of the tape are in contact with each surface to create a strong and permanent bond.</p><p>The market for double-sided tape has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. This can be attributed to its wide range of applications across various industries, such as automotive, electronics, packaging, construction, and medical. The increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials in these industries is driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the key trends in the double-sided tape market is the rising adoption of acrylic-based tapes. Acrylic adhesives provide excellent bonding strength and durability, making them suitable for long-term applications. They also offer resistance to temperature, UV radiation, and chemicals, making them ideal for outdoor applications.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing demand for low-VOC and environmentally friendly tapes.

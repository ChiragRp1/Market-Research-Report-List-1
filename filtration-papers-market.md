<p><ul><li>Qualitative Filtration Papers</li><li>Quantitative Filtration Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Filtration Papers market can be classified into two main types: Qualitative Filtration Papers and Quantitative Filtration Papers. Qualitative Filtration Papers are used when the aim is to separate particles based on size only, without determining the exact quantity. These papers are commonly used in laboratories for qualitative analysis. On the other hand, Quantitative Filtration Papers are used when both size separation and precise measurement of the quantity of particles are required. These papers are used for quantitative analysis in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing.</p></p>

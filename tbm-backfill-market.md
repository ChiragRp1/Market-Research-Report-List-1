Soft Ground TBMs are specifically designed for excavating tunnels in loose soil, clay, or sand formations. They use soil conditioning and face support systems to minimize ground settlement. On the other hand, Hard Rock TBMs are engineered for tunneling through solid rock formations. They employ disc cutters and hydraulic fracturing techniques to break and remove the rock as they advance. Both types of TBMs play a crucial role in underground construction projects, ensuring efficient and safe excavation in their respective ground conditions.</p></p>

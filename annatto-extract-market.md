<p><p>Annatto extract, also known as achiote extract, is derived from the seeds of the achiote tree (Bixa orellana). It is widely used as a natural food coloring agent due to its bright orange-red color, which is primarily attributed to the presence of carotenoid pigments such as bixin and norbixin. Annatto extract is commonly used in various food and beverage applications, including dairy products, bakery and confectionery, snacks, and beverages.</p><p>The global annatto extract market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for natural food colorants, coupled with the rising consumer preference for clean-label products, is driving the market growth. Annatto extract offers several advantages, such as ease of use, stability, and cost-effectiveness, compared to other synthetic food colorants, which further fuels its market demand.</p><p>Additionally, the growing awareness among consumers regarding the health risks associated with synthetic food colorants, such as allergies and hyperactivity, is also boosting the demand for natural alternatives like annatto extract.

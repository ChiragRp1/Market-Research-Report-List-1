<p><p>The dry snuff market comprises two main types: loose snus and portion snus. Loose snus refers to powdered tobacco that users scoop and form into a pouch, placing it under their lip. It allows for customization in terms of quantity used. On the other hand, portion snus comes in pre-portioned sachets, ready for direct placement under the lip. This concept offers convenience and consistency in size and strength of the product. Both loose snus and portion snus cater to individuals seeking smokeless alternatives, providing unique options in the dry snuff market.</p></p>

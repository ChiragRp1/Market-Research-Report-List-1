Level 2 Charging utilizes a 240-volt charger, providing a faster charging rate and often installed at homes or workplaces for convenience. Level 3 Charging, also known as DC fast charging, offers the highest charging rate and is typically found in public spaces or along highways, enabling rapid charging of electric vehicles.</p></p>

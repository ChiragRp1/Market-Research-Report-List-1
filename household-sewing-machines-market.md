<p><p>Household sewing machines can be categorized into two market types: mechanical sewing machines and electronic sewing machines. Mechanical sewing machines are operated manually with the use of a foot pedal and rely on mechanical gears and levers for stitching. On the other hand, electronic sewing machines are powered by electricity and offer advanced features like automatic threading, adjustable stitch lengths, and a variety of preset stitches. They also often include a digital display and buttons for easy navigation and customization.</p></p>

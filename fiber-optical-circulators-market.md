<p><p>Fiber optic circulators are devices used in optical communication networks to direct light signals in specific directions. They can be categorized based on the number of ports they have. A 3-port circulator has three ports, allowing light to travel in a unidirectional manner from one port to the next. Meanwhile, a 4-port circulator has an extra port, enabling bidirectional transmission of light. Both types are crucial for efficient and reliable signal routing in various applications, such as telecommunications, data centers, and sensing systems.</p></p>

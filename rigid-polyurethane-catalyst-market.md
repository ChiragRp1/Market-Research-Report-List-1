<p><strong>The Rigid Polyurethane Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rigid Polyurethane Catalyst market is divided into two main types: Non-reactive Amine Catalysts and Reactive Amine Catalysts. </p><p>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts are substances that do not participate in the chemical reaction but enhance the reaction rate and efficiency. They promote the curing process of rigid polyurethane by accelerating its reaction with other components.</p><p>On the other hand, Reactive Amine Catalysts actively participate in the chemical reaction. They not only accelerate the reaction rate but also chemically react with the polyurethane components, leading to the formation of a crosslinked network structure and improving the overall strength and stability of rigid polyurethane products.</p></p>

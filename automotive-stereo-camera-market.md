<p><strong>The Automotive Stereo Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Stereo Camera</li><li>Static Stereo Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive stereo camera market consists of two main types: dynamic stereo camera and static stereo camera. A dynamic stereo camera provides real-time 3D imaging by continuously adjusting its focus and capturing multiple exposures. It is mainly used for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and autonomous vehicles to accurately perceive the surroundings and make timely decisions. On the other hand, a static stereo camera captures a single exposure for 3D imaging and is commonly used for applications such as lane departure warning, traffic sign recognition, and pedestrian detection.</p></p>

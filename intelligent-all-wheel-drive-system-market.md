<p><p>The Intelligent All-Wheel Drive (AWD) System is categorized into three types based on the default drive configuration. In Front Wheel Drive by Default, power is primarily directed to the front wheels, enhancing fuel efficiency in normal driving conditions but can redirect power to rear wheels when additional traction is required. In Four Wheel Drive by Default, power is evenly distributed to all four wheels, providing enhanced off-road and slippery surface capabilities at all times. In Rear Wheel Drive by Default, power is directed to the rear wheels, offering better handling for performance-oriented vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>Film Grade PLA is a biodegradable polymer widely used in packaging and film applications. Two market types of Film Grade PLA are Translucent Film and Opaque Film. Translucent Film has a semi-transparent appearance, allowing some light to pass through while maintaining visibility. It is commonly used in products like food packaging or greenhouse films. On the other hand, Opaque Film is completely blockout, preventing any light penetration. This type is utilized in applications such as light-blocking or privacy films, where complete darkness or opacity is desired.</p></p>

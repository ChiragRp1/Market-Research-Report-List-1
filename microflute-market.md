<p><p>Microflute is a type of packaging material composed of multiple layers of fluted paper. The market for microflute can be categorized into three types: E Type, F Type, and N Type. E Type microflute is the most common, characterized by thinner flute size and lower stacking strength. F Type microflute is the strongest, with thicker flute size and higher stacking strength, suitable for heavy objects. N Type microflute offers a compromise, providing moderate stacking strength and flute size. These market types cater to different packaging needs depending on the weight and fragility of the products.</p></p>

<p><p>The Ceramic Textile market consists of three main types: RCF (Refractory Ceramic Fiber), Low Bio-Persistent, and Polycrystalline. RCF is a commonly used insulation material due to its high-temperature resistance and lightweight properties. Low Bio-Persistent textiles offer a safer alternative with reduced health risks compared to RCF. Polycrystalline textiles are known for their exceptional mechanical strength and high electrical resistance. These market types cater to various industries, such as aerospace, automotive, and energy, providing solutions for insulation, filtration, and fire protection applications.</p></p>

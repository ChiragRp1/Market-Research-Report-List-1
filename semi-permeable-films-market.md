<p><strong>The Semi Permeable Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biomembrane</li><li>Synthetic Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-permeable films refer to a type of material that allows certain substances to pass through while blocking others. Biomembranes are semi-permeable films made from natural materials like lipid bilayers found in biological systems. They are used in applications such as drug delivery and tissue engineering. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are artificially made films that imitate the properties of natural biomembranes. These membranes find applications in water treatment, gas separation, and medical devices. Both biomembranes and synthetic membranes play crucial roles in various industries due to their selective permeability properties.</p></p>

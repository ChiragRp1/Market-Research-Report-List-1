<p><p>The high performance fluoropolymer market comprises four major types of fluoropolymers: Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene (FEP), Perfluoroalkoxy Polymer (PFA), Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), and Polyethylenetetrafluoroethylene (ETFE). FEP is known for its excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it suitable for applications in wire and cable insulation, cookware, and tubing. PFA offers similar properties but has a higher temperature resistance, making it ideal for semiconductor manufacturing and automotive parts. PTFE provides exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it widely used in industrial applications such as lining pipes and manufacturing gaskets. ETFE is valued for its high strength and transparency, making it popular in architectural applications like roofing and facades.</p></p>

<p><p>Mixed phenol, also known as phenol mixture or phenolic fraction, is a byproduct of crude oil refining and coal tar distillation processes. The rising demand for resins and plastics, particularly in the construction and automotive sectors, is driving the market growth. Mixed phenol is widely used in the production of epoxy, phenolic, and polyester resins, which find applications in coatings, adhesives, electrical insulation, and composites.</p><p>Moreover, the growing healthcare sector and increasing demand for pharmaceuticals are also contributing to the market growth. Mixed phenol is used in the synthesis of drugs, as well as in the production of antiseptics, disinfectants, and preservatives.</p><p>In terms of market analysis and trends, the Asia-Pacific region is expected to dominate the mixed phenol market.

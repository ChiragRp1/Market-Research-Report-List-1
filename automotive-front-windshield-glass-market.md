As vehicle customization trends continue to rise, front windshields are expected to become a key area for innovation and aesthetic enhancements. Manufacturers are actively exploring smart glass technologies that can change opacity, display information, and regulate light transmission to improve driving experience and safety.</p><p>Additionally, the growing popularity of connected vehicles and the integration of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) are expected to drive the demand for front windshield glass with sensors for rain detection, light intensity adjustment, and other functionalities.

<p><p>Defrosting and thawing equipment for food is available in various market types, including chambers, tumblers, and continuous lines. Chambers are enclosed spaces where food products are placed to defrost under controlled temperature and humidity conditions. Tumblers consist of rotating drums that help evenly distribute heat and thaw food. Continuous lines, on the other hand, are conveyor belt systems that allow for a constant flow of food products to be defrosted and thawed. Each of these market types offers unique benefits and features to cater to different industry needs for safe and effective food defrosting.</p></p>

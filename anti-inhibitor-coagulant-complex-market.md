<p><p>Anti-inhibitor coagulant complex (AICC) is a blood product used in the treatment of patients with hemophilia A and B. AICC contains clotting factors that can bypass the inhibitors and promote normal blood clotting.</p><p>The future outlook for the AICC market is promising.

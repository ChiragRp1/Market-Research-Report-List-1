<p><p>Metal cleaners can be broadly categorized into two types: alkaline cleaners and acid cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are formulated with alkaline substances like sodium hydroxide or potassium hydroxide. These cleaners are generally used for removing organic contaminants and light residues from metal surfaces. On the other hand, acid cleaners contain acids like hydrochloric acid or sulfuric acid. They are primarily used for eliminating inorganic deposits or heavy stains on metal surfaces. Both alkaline and acid cleaners play crucial roles in maintaining the cleanliness and appearance of various metal objects and surfaces.</p></p>

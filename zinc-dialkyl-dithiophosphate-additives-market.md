<p><p>Zinc Dialkyl Dithiophosphate (ZDDP) additives are commonly used in lubricants for their anti-wear and anti-oxidation properties. The market for these additives can be divided into two types: Primary and Secondary ZDDP additives. Primary ZDDP additives are used directly in the formulation of lubricants to improve their performance and protect engine parts under extreme conditions. On the other hand, Secondary ZDDP additives are used as boosters in the formulation of lubricants that already contain primary ZDDP additives, allowing for enhanced performance and protection. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and longevity of lubricants.</p></p>

They offer flexibility and cost-effectiveness for smaller-scale production environments. On the other hand, fully automatic dispensers are capable of performing dispensing operations without any human assistance. They are suitable for high-volume manufacturing, ensuring efficiency and precision in dispensing materials onto semiconductors. SiPs combine multiple semiconductor chips and passive components into a single package.

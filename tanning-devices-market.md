<p><p>Tanning devices market consists of three main types: lamps, beds, and booths. Lamps refer to the individual light bulbs used to tan the skin, commonly found in home tanning kits. Beds, on the other hand, are larger machines that provide a full-body tanning experience, allowing individuals to lay down and receive UV exposure all over their body. Booths are similar to beds but are enclosed spaces where users stand upright to receive a more convenient and efficient tanning session. Each of these devices aims to provide artificial UV radiation to achieve a tan-like appearance on the skin.</p></p>

<p><p>A parking brake shoe is an essential component of a parking brake system in vehicles. It is responsible for applying the necessary friction to the brake drum or rotor, thus preventing the vehicle from rolling when parked. The parking brake shoe consists of a set of curved metal or composite plates lined with friction material, which is pressed against the brake drum or rotor when the parking brake is engaged.</p><p>The future outlook of the parking brake shoe market appears promising. The market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecasted period, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.4%.

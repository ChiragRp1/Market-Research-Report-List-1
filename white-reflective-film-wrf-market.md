<p><p>White Reflective Film (WRF) is categorized into two main market types: Non-metallic Films and Metallic Films. Non-metallic films are made from materials that do not contain metal elements and offer excellent reflectivity by utilizing various pigments and coatings. They are commonly used in signage, displays, and architectural applications. On the other hand, Metallic Films incorporate metal layers that enhance reflectivity and reduce heat transfer. This type of film is widely employed in automotive, aerospace, and building industries, where high reflectivity and durability are required for heat management and energy efficiency purposes.</p></p>
